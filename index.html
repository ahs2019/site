Field Trip to Wamesit Lanesclose

To celebrate the end of the school year, there will be a freshman only class trip to Wamesit Lanes Entertainment Center in Tewksbury on Friday, June 3 from 11:30am - 3:00pm. There's a huge bowling alley, big arcade room, virtual reality golf simulators, their exclusive Lazer Frenzy Experience, pizza/drinks, and a blast to be had with their friends! The total cost for all of this is $31.00. Everyone must go on the buses provided to and from the trip.

Spots can be reserved on the trip all this week (May 23-27) by purchasing admission in their lunches at the Freshman Board table in the cafe. Students can also see either Mr. Abbott or Mr. Duncan in the Guidance office. Cash or checks (made payable to "Andover High School") are accepted, and all students must also have a signed parent consent field trip form. Additional copies can be found on the counter in the guidance department.
